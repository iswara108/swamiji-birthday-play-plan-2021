The game of dice
5 minutes
Śakuni is showing the new hall to the Pāṇḍavas and invites them to the game. The game is afoot and he is winning every time. Yudhiṣṭhira loses his sanity and puts everything on the table, Draupadī is lost.
5
Song - Upaniṣad - Draupadī and Duśāsana
3 minutes
Duśāsana meets Draupadī and tell her she is now the slave of the Kauravas.
Draupadī argues back and the Upaniṣad song emerges (based on the melody of America, West Side Story).
Duśāsana tries to undress Draupadī.
Bhīma vows to take revenge.
6
Final game and deportation
4 minutes
Śakuni offers Yudhiṣṭhira one more tossing of the dice, if they win they get it all back, if they lose they are exiled for 12 years + 1 incognito.
The Pāṇḍavas walk out into exile.
7
Back to the lake of death - the brothers die at the hands of the yakśa
3 minutes
All the brothers finish their reminisce, Yudhiṣṭhira says that right now they have to solve their immediate problem: Thirst and hunger. Sends Nakula in search for water.

Ballet: Nakula, Sahadeva, Arjuna and Bhīma go one by one to the lake, look around, surprised to see their dead brothers, get near the lake, hear a voice, surprised by the warning, ignoring the warning, dring and die.
Yudhiṣṭhira finally arrives and is shocked to see his brothers lying dead.
Background music: In the Mountain Hall of the Mountain King, Peer Gynt.
8
Song - Oh Yakśa
3 minutes, 30 seconds
Song by Yudhiṣṭhira and the dead brothers about the possibility of returning to life.
(Based on the melody of Matchmaker, Fiddler on the Roof).
9
Yakśa Praśna
5 minutes
The yakśa appears before Yudhiṣṭhira and praises him for his behavior. Proceeds to ask him questions.
Questions are asked and Yudhiṣṭhira replies. The topics are dharma, vedānta, general knowledge, a riddle.
The yakśa grants a boon to restore one of the brothers to life. Yudhiṣṭhira chooses Nakula and the yakśa is surprised. Yudhiṣṭhira explains the moral reason and the yakśa brings all brothers to life. Yudhiṣṭhira exposes the yakśa as his father, dharma and dharma blesses all of them before the year of incognito.

Scenery: The forest

Scene 1: Introduction of Characters

Duration: 3 minutes

Over the background music: Good Morning, Peer Gynt, the five pāṇḍavas and Draupadī greet each other good morning and shortly explain the background, where and who they are.
At the end, the priest frantically knocks on the door and complains that Kāśī stole his sticks for fire. The Pāṇḍavas hastily go after her to help the priest.

Scene 2: The chase after Kāśī - film

Duration: 2 minutes

Show a video of the chase.
The chase involves ~5 second shots alternating between Kāśī and the Pāṇḍavas chasing her, along with the music of Anrita's Dance, Peer Gynt. Each 4 bars constitue one shot. Altogether, around 11 pairs of shots. At the end, Kāśī manages to escape.

Scene 3: Lake of death - blaming the past

Duration: 2 minutes

The Pāṇḍavas helplessly give up the chase and find a place to sit together. They ask each other why this has happened to them, and each one giving some reasons, ultimtely remembering the game of dice, which is elaborated in the next scenes as a reminisce.
